2010 Masters Golf Pool
                                                       April 5 - 11, 2010

The 2010 Tiger Woods Return-a-Palooza will shortly be upon us, and in the middle of it all will be the minor item of a golf tournament. That would be the first major of the year, the 2010 Masters. Yes, he left us hanging for quite some time about when he would next appear. Yours truly even predicted he’d sit it out and work on his, ahhh, addictions till Pebble Beach. Turns out his transgressions were healed/forgiven in a far shorter period of time, luckily enough for us golf and poolie fans.

Welcome to the pool. Same ol’, same ol. Minor, minor modification to the rules – more of review of a possible scenario – check it out here if you’re interested. Enter your team and enjoy the golf, now with confirmed Tiger content. Fore!
Congratulations to Phil Mickelson and his third green jacket!

Congrats to Thomas MacKinnon, Drive for Show, Pool Champ!
