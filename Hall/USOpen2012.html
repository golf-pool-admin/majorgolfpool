Webb Simpson 2012 US Open Champ

            
Latest Pool News: June 18, 2012 - 9:00 AM. Congrats to Webb Simpson, the 2012 US Open champ. Two 68s on weekend, not surprisingly, led him to the top of the podium. I really thought Jim Furyk, the plodder, veteran, and former champ was going to get it done on the early holes, but by the turn and nary a birdie in site, I was a little worried. A great tournmanent, though. Lots of interest from Ernie and Padraig; Graeme stayed in there, and how ‘bout those Cypress trees?

To the pool! Congrats to Barry Thompson’s team, The Bear, and his first pool win! His picks of Graeme, Jim, Padraig, and Ernie led him to victory. In solo second, team Markakos, takes the honours. And a tie for third between The Carl Spacklers and  Jay’s Slashers …. Winning score pick comes into play here and the gopher hunters (-5) triumph over the slashers (-10).
