It’s been a brilliant and  entertaining start to the golf season this year. And I must say – although I’d like to see him back at the top – I’m not missing Tiger. We’ve seen some great play by Phil at Pebble and Rivieria, the young guns Stanley, Bradley, Haas, Bae, and Rory especially are really coming on and showing great poise. Even Mike Weir made a cut. Golf is definitely bigger than Tiger (not sure if that’s true for TV ratings.)
 
Anyway, I hope this bodes well for an exciting and dramatic major at Augusta this year. And to add just a dash more interest, the pool is up, too! Get your picks in now! Fore!
