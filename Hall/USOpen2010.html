2010 US Open Golf Pool
Pebble Beach, California
June 14-20, 2010

What will be the state of Tiger's health, game, and mental state as he approaches the site of one of his most triumphant major victories, the 2000 US Open? His game is looking rather pedestrian lately, with Phil close to nabbing top spot in the world. Anyway, Pebble’s a great venue and Tiger will probably step up. Lots of interesting stories to unfold, including who will be the next US Open Golf Pool champ! Get your entries in now. Fore!

Latest Pool News: June 20, 2010 - 10:30 PM.  Wow, what a tourney. Congrats to Graeme McDowell on winning his first major. A well-deserved win for the northern Irishman. And kudos to Greg Havret who hung in there; Tiger had some  nice words to say about your play after the tourney. Ernie and Phil had a chance, but it was a tough course out there today, and, well, all week.

To the pool: Um, yes, it finally happened. Yours truly, Brian Duffield, World Cup Schmurld Cup, has won the pool. It never looks good when the guy running the pool wins it, but hey, it’s kinda my first one in 15 tries. Perhaps the bigger story is who came in third place: Tom Currie’s team, Who Els?, finally after 15 pools, makes it into the money. Congrats, Tom. A well deserved place. And let’s not forget second place. team CHIMPO, Chima Mclean’s team.

I’ll double check everything tomorrow as I’ve had a few adult beverages. Happy father’s day all! Fore!.
