2013 PGA Championship Golf Pool
Oak Hill Country Club
Rochester, New York
August 5-11, 2013



Here we go ... the pool slam! Glory's last shot for both the pros and poolies. Seems like ages ago that Rory was number one in the world and poised for major dominance. And now he seems unlikely to even make the cut these days. Well, he's gotta snap out of it sooner or later and a repeat Wanamaker would certainly get the ball rolling.

And how 'bout Phil? Could he finally jump ahead of Tiger and claim the number one spot in the world with another major victory? Or can Tiger solve his putting and final round woes and join the major winners this year. As always, should be fun to find out.

The pool is on. Get your entries in now for the last pool of the year. Good luck! Fore!

Jason Dufner 2013 PGA Champ

                                   
Latest Pool News: August 11, 2013  - 9:00 PM. Congratulations to Jason Dufner on his first major championship. Redemptions and all that, yada yada. It was pretty much match play after 9 holes, and Jim’s iron play just couldn’t match Dufner’s, not by a long shot. And although Jim made some nice putts, he couldn’t put the pressure on. Too bad we didn’t get a run from someone behind, but all-in-all not a bad finish to the year’s final major.

To the pool, and congratulations to Candice Blake’s team, Candy’s Guys. She nipped 3rd round leader, Sean Shannon’s Rochester Rockets! by one stroke. And it was indeed all of Candy’s guys who contributed to her win. Sean had the top 4 finishers on his team, but the other half couldn’t match up to Candice’s deep bench. This pool is definitely a ‘team’ game; check out the details of both of their teams on the Round 4 results page. Congrats also go out to Andrew Stanfield’s team, linsmore, who takes third all alone.

I did double-check the scores, so, I think everything looks right. Let me know if you see anything amiss. Again, all team and player scores are available here or at the link below.

Hope everyone enjoyed the pool slam this year. Can’t guarantee it’ll happen next year, but we’ll see. Enjoy the rest of your summer folks. Cheers!  More news here.
