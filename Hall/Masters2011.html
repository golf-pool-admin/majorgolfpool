2011 Masters Golf Pool
                                                       April 7 - 10, 2011

What a difference a year makes. At this time last year, Tiger and Phil were well ensconced in the top five in the world. As of this writing, Tiger is 5th (!) and Phil is right behind him, which makes for a very interesting story you won’t find on the Golf Channel: depending on the results after Bay Hill, Tiger and Phil may both find themselves out of the top category in my pool entry form. Or they may split categories allowing poolies the first opportunity ever to pick both Tiger and Phil. Of course, lots of other great players and young guns to choose from in those categories. Will you stick with the old guard or lay your bets with the new? By the time most of you are reading this, the answer will be known, so, click on this year’s entry form and make your picks! Fore!
Congrats to Charl Schwartzel on his first major!
Congrats to Sean Shannon on his 2nd pool victory!

Charl Schwartzel 2011 Masters Champ

                    
Latest Pool News:  April 10,  2011 - 9:00 PM. An amazing final round that had me pretty riveted to the action, especially after Tiger’s 2nd on 8. Tough to see Rory fall back, but I predict we’ll see him contending for another major pretty soon. And congrats to Charl Schwartzel for a solid first major win. I’ve been following Charl for some time ever since I first discovered his name wasn’t a typo (and had him on my team this year.)  Four birdies on the last four holes at Augusta deserves a green jacket.

To the pool, and we have another two-time champion. Congrats to Sean Shannon’s team Orange County. Sean won the 2009 PGA pool, and can now add the coveted green jacket to his trophy case. Sean’s team didn’t have Charl, but did have Adam, Jason, Luke, and Ogilvy to give him his commanding lead at -58. Second place goes to Barry Weir’s team Line ‘em Up; and third goes to Dean Pinkham’s Dormy Dude. Congrats to our winners; all teams results are here. I’ll double-check and confirm all tomorrow.
