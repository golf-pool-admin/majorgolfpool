2006 Masters Golf Pool

The first official (golf) sign of spring is here: The 2006 Masters is mere days away. And, of course, so is my Masters Pool! I’ve enhanced the Web site a bit and updated the online form entry process by using a third-party forms company. See the Rules and Info link for full details. I’ve run two pools now and Tiger has won each time (the tournament, not the pool). Can he make it three? Can Mike Weir string together four decent rounds? Let’s hope so. And will Hootie Johnson make Sergio, Adam, and Tim get a haircut? We’ll find out Masters week. Any questions or comments about the pool, contact masterspool2006@yahoo.com. Fore!
