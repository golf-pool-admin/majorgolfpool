Welcome to my 5th and, so far, annual Masters golf pool. This pool I'm keeping things simple and generally maintaining the status quo. I had hoped to add a few more gizmos to the site, but timing is a bit of an issue these days. That said, a few updates: I've added a few new rules (nothing really of import, mainly housekeeping), and a few pics of the boys. Looking forward to the tourney! Spring is here (hopefully); Tiger is back; Mike is playing decent; and I've got a new TV! Enjoy this year's Masters and the pool. Fore!

Latest Pool News:  April 12, 2009: 9:00 PM. Wow, it felt like two golf tournaments out there today. The Tiger/Phil show was something else, and looked to erase the final two groups from the telecast. Until 17 and 18. And then when Kenny hit that beautiful shot on 16, I thought he had the green jacket sewn up. Until 17 and 18. And how Cabrara came back from that stymie at the first playoff hole is unbelievable. Just goes to show you: never give up. Congrats to Angel Cabrara on his second major victory.

To the pool … out of no where (actually 13th after the 3rd) Steve Siuta roars to his first pool win. Steve had a deep bench on his team, including Stricker, Flesch, and Merrick. Coming in second, Tom Barry’s puttfordough, our first round leader. And placing was Heinz with his team 57. Congrats to our winners. And thanks to all for a great pool this year.
