Alright. To get yourself in the U.S. Open mood, click the "Watch U.S. Open Trailer" from the official U.S. Open site here, turn up the sound, and sit back and enjoy. It starts off like a cheesy Hollywood movie, deep church choirs behind dramatic strings, but then, when the golfers come on screen and the music crescendos and the putts start to fall to bursts of timpani, well, oh my, I would've charged up San Juan hill after that. Take a few moments to recover, then click on the entry form below and predict who will triumph over man and deep rough at this year's 2nd major. Good luck.

Latest Pool News:  June 23, 2008 -7:30 AM:  A late update wrapping things up. Congrats to Tiger Woods and his extra hole win over a plucky Rocco Mediate. Hopefully, Tiger's knee can make a comeback in 2009.

And after all the ballots were recounted, Mr. Ian McLean, of eastern Ontario, team Woody Takes a Hack, wins his first major pool! Congrats, Ian. A final round of apologies to Mr. Dean Pinkham, Dormy Dude, the first proclaimed winner, but eventual runner up. However, a solid 2nd place showing for Dean to add to his trophy list. Congrats also to Marion Deluca, Cute Pinky, our third place winner. Thanks for playing all. Fore!
