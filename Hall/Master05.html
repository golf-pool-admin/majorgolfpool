Latest Pool News:

April 11th, 2005: Congrats again to Sanjay Gupta's Team, King Kumar and the Lords of Golf. He picked both Tiger and Chris DiMarco. But that wasn't enough. He also needed the winnings from Thomas Levet (only entrant to pick Levet), Chad Campbell, and Bernhard Langer to outlast Mark McLean's Bird Dogs. Mike Weir's bogey-bogey-bogey finish might've cost Mark first place. And congrats to all you Tiger believers. After round one I didn't expect to see Tiger in Butler cabin. Commiserations to all fellow Ernie fans. Apparently he was suffering from the "flu". Maybe next year, Ernie. Thanks to all for participating. Cheers. Brian.

April 11th, 2005: I've updated individual team winnings. Please check your teams to make sure the addition and purse distribution is correct. I've used a couple of sources to verify the purse, but if you see any discrepancies, please let me know.
